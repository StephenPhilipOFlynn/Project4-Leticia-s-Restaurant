Our Story

Welcome to Leticia's, a culinary gem nesteld in the heart of Dublin City, where the vibrant flavours of Brazil and Spain are brought to life.

Our story begins with Leticia, our founder and head chef, whose childhood in Sao Paulo and sun-kissed holidays in Southern Brazil ignited her love for Brazilian cuisine.

Leticia's culinary love was further enriched by her Spainish grandparents, whose stories and recipes from Spain add a unique Galician flair to her cooking.
